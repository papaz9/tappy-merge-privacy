Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Cool9Games ("we," "us," or "our") built Noor ("the App") as a free mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this policy carefully. By downloading or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you use Noor, certain information may be collected automatically, including but not limited to:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and language settings.
  • Usage Data: App interaction data such as feature usage, session duration, and crash/performance logs.
  • Location Data: Approximate location (city-level) used solely for determining accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. This data is processed on-device and is not stored on our servers.

1.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

Noor does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.) unless voluntarily provided
  • Religious beliefs or practices data transmitted to external servers
  • Contacts, photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Financial or payment information (handled entirely by app store platforms)
  • Precise GPS coordinates sent to our servers

3. How We Use Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Prayer Time Calculation: To determine accurate prayer times based on your approximate location.
  • Qibla Direction: To calculate the direction of Qibla using device sensors and location.
  • App Functionality: To save your preferences, dhikr counts, bookmarks, and settings locally on your device.
  • App Improvement: To identify and fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues through anonymous analytics.
  • Subscription Management: To manage premium subscription status through RevenueCat.

4. Data Storage and Security

All user data (prayer settings, dhikr counts, bookmarks, preferences) is stored locally on your device only using secure local storage. This data is not transmitted to our servers or any third party.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect any information processed through third-party services. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

  • Local Data: App data is stored on your device until you uninstall the App or clear the App's data through your device settings.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage data collected by Firebase Analytics is retained according to Google's data retention policies.
  • Subscription Data: Subscription status data is managed by RevenueCat and the respective app store (Google Play / App Store).

6. Children's Privacy

Noor is a general-audience application suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us at the email address below, and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data:

7.1 All Users

  • Location Access: You can disable location access for Noor in your device settings. Prayer times can still be set manually.
  • Delete Local Data: Uninstall the App or clear App data from your device settings.
  • Notification Control: You can enable or disable prayer time notifications in the App settings or your device settings.

7.2 European Economic Area (EEA) Users — GDPR

If you are located in the EEA, you have the following additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

The legal basis for processing your data is legitimate interest (providing accurate prayer times) and consent (where applicable).

7.3 California Users — CCPA

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of any sale of personal information. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
