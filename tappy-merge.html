Cool9Games ("we," "us," or "our") built Tappy Merge ("the App") as a free, ad-supported mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this policy carefully. By downloading or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you use Tappy Merge, certain information may be collected automatically, including but not limited to:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information, and language settings.
  • Usage Data: App interaction data such as game scores, session duration, feature usage, and crash/performance logs.
  • Advertising Data: Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID on Android, IDFA on iOS), ad interaction data (impressions, clicks), and IP address (used for approximate location for ad targeting at the country/region level only).

1.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

Tappy Merge does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Precise geolocation data (GPS coordinates)
  • Contacts, photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Financial or payment information
  • User-generated content or messages

3. How We Use Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Game Functionality: To save your game progress, best scores, and preferences locally on your device using SharedPreferences.
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob, which supports the free availability of the App.
  • App Improvement: To identify and fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues.
  • Analytics: To understand general usage patterns and improve the user experience.

4. Data Storage and Security

All game-related data (scores, game state, preferences, sound settings) is stored locally on your device only using SharedPreferences. This data is not transmitted to our servers or any third party.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect any information processed through third-party services. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Game data is stored on your device until you uninstall the App or clear the App's data through your device settings.
  • Third-Party Data: Data collected by Google AdMob is retained according to Google's data retention policies.

6. Children's Privacy

Tappy Merge is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us at the email address below, and we will take steps to delete such information.

In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations, our ad implementation follows Google's policies regarding ad content for mixed-audience apps.
